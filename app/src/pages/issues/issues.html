Known Issues

Scanning Barcodes

The barcode scanner does not work well when scanning bright screens. If you are having trouble scanning hackers' QR codes, please ask them to reduce their screen brightness.

Accessing the Scanner

When you try to access the camera, the app only checks whether it already has permission to access the camera. If it doesn't, it will simply abort the scan. Before you use the scanner for the first time, please give the app permission to access the camera.
